Elevator Pitch(place holder):
BingeBuddy! because Pirating is Illegal!

Problem Description:
with an over abundance of streaming services, especially ones that require a subscription to view exclusive shows and movies, it becomes difficult to track and keep track of a users favorite shows and updates to them. it also becomes a hassel as it would also require users to sift through multiple services to find a show that might peak their interest, taking up time and could either annoy or even drive users away from certain platforms as a result

Customers:
those with multiple subscriptions to streaming services and those looking for reccomendations both in their existing subscriptions as well as other services they are not yet subscribed to.

Solution:
binge buddy would take user data as well as tags/serivice data from the streaming services to better reccomend as well as keep track of shows the users wish to follow, resulting in a streamlined experience for the user to search, gain reccomendations, as well as keep up to date with their existing tagged or followed shows.
